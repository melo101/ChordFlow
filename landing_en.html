About ChordFlow

ChordFlow is a free and ad-free application that allows you to organize songs, chords, and lyrics in a simple, quick, and practical way. Developed by an amateur musician with a profession outside the technology field, the app was born from a real need on stage — and evolved with feedback from other musicians.

With two viewing modes (chords or complete lyrics with chords), it's ideal for rehearsals, live performances, or individual study. The interface is lightweight, intuitive, and optimized to work well on any device, even without an internet connection.

As it is developed by just one person, the app may still have some limitations or minor bugs. There are other apps on the market with more advanced features, but they can also be more complex and less practical on stage. ChordFlow aims to be a lightweight, intuitive and essential solution.

Available Versions

Android

Optimized for stage use, rehearsals or studio sessions, presenting chords and sections clearly and accessibly.

Windows

Designed as a creation and editing assistant — ideal for comfortably preparing your setlists on a computer before your concerts.

Why the Windows version was created

Creating long setlists on a phone or tablet can be slow and impractical — especially with dozens of songs. The Windows version of ChordFlow was created to solve this:

  • Edit songs and chords more quickly and comfortably
  • Organize setlists using your mouse and keyboard
  • Export easily to the Android app

This way, you prepare everything on your PC and take only the essentials to the stage.

Purpose Summary:

  • Create and edit musical setlists with organized sections
  • Manage chords and song structure
  • Use the Windows version as a support tool
  • Use the Android version for live consultation on stage or rehearsal
