What is ChordFlow for?

ChordFlow is an application designed to help musicians create, organize and manage their setlists in a practical, intuitive and distraction-free way.

The Android version is optimized for use on stage, in rehearsals or in the studio, presenting chords and song sections in a clear and accessible format.

The Windows version is intended as a creation and editing assistant â€” ideal for comfortably preparing songs and setlists on a computer before a concert. You can structure sections, edit chords and export everything to the Android app, where the setlists are used in real performance contexts.

Creating long setlists on a tablet or phone can be tedious â€” especially when you're dealing with dozens of songs. That's why we built ChordFlow for Windows:

  • Organize your setlists using keyboard and mouse
  • Export everything to the Android app in a few clicks

This way, you prepare everything on your PC and bring only the essentials to the stage.
