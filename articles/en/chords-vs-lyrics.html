Chords by Section vs Lyrics with Chords in ChordFlow

ChordFlow supports two practical song formats. The best choice depends on how much lyrical detail you need and how you prefer to read songs on stage.

Download on Google Play

When to Use Chords by Section

The section-based editor works well when you want a compact performance view with a clear structure like Intro, Verse, Chorus, and Bridge.

  • Great for players who already know the lyrics.
  • Excellent for rehearsals focused on structure and harmony.
  • Keeps the screen shorter and easier to scan from a distance.

When to Use Lyrics with Chords

The lyrics-with-chords layout is better when vocal cues, exact phrasing, or chord placement over words matters during performance.

Choose Based on the Performance Context

If you mainly need structure, use sections. If you need the exact words on screen, use lyrics with chords. You can also keep different songs in different formats inside the same repertoire.
